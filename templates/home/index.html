Our Services

Information on Hemophilia

We provide complete information on Hemophilia and its associated condition. Beneficiaries can avail information to channelize their life style towards healthy outcome

Patient Care Services

Out-patient and in-patient services for people with hemophilia. We are also empanelled with State Government to procure and distribute factors for patients

Counselling

We offer psychosocial and counselling services for the family to overcome the common stigma associated with hemophilia.

Physiotherapy

Disability is one of the complications of uncontrolled hemophilia. We help the disabled to strengthen their joints and muscle to overcome repeated bleeding.
